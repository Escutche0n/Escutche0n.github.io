### 交通

##### 前程

- 欧洲之星

- Bristol to Grenoble airport—direct flight on Dec 22nd 

- Shuttle bus or private transfer

- Or rent a car (7 days?)

###### 返程

- Shuttle bus or private transfer

- Drive to Geneva or Lyon or Turin (note: car rental in different countries?)

- Or drive to Lyon, return the car, take TER or Flixbus to Geneva
