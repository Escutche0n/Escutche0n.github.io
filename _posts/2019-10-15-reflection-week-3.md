### **Briefs**



##### IDOART 

**Intention** – What is the intention, or purpose, of the meeting? In other words, why have it? 

**Desired Outcome(s**) – What specific outcomes should be achieved by the end of the meeting? 

**Agenda** – What activities will you go through, in what order, to move toward the desired outcome? 

**Roles** – What roles or responsibilities need to be in place for the meeting to run smoothly? 

*Who is facilitating, and who is participating? Who is documenting, and who is keeping track of the time?* 

**Rules** – What guidelines will be in place during the meeting? These could relate to agreed group norms and behaviours. They could also relate to use of laptops/mobiles, or practical rules related to a space. 

**Time** – What is the expected time for the meeting, including breaks, and at what time will the meeting end?
